Welcome to Verbal Vortex

Verbal Vortex is inspired by the classic word-guessing game hangman, in which one player thinks of a word and the other player attempts to guess it letter by letter. The goal is to guess the word before your guesses are completely gone. It's a quick and engaging challenge that tests your vocabulary and deduction abilities.
